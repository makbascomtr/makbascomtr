Personal Data

Protection and Management of Personal Data

Makbaş LTD. ŞTİ. considers the protection of personal data as a top priority and takes all necessary measures in this regard. The policies we implement to ensure the security of your personal data and protect your privacy are outlined below:

Data Collection and Use

Personal data is collected only within the legal framework and for specific purposes. The data we collect may include:

  • Contact information (name, surname, email address, phone number)

  • Information about transactions and purchases made with our company

  • Technical data collected when you visit our website (IP address, browser information)

Data Security

We take necessary technical and administrative measures to ensure the security of your personal data. We implement various security protocols to protect your data from unauthorized access, use, alteration, or loss.

Data Retention

We only retain your personal data for as long as necessary and in compliance with legal requirements. The retention period of your data is determined according to relevant legislation and business requirements.

Data Sharing

Your personal data may only be shared with third parties with whom we collaborate to fulfill our legal obligations or to provide our services. Necessary security measures are taken before sharing your data, and your privacy is protected.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access your data

  • Right to rectify your data

  • Right to delete your data

  • Right to object to data processing activities

You can contact us to exercise these rights or for more information.
