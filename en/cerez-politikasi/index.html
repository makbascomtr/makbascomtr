Cookie Policy

Use and Management of Cookies

Makbaş LTD. ŞTİ. aims to enhance the user experience on our website by using cookies. This cookie policy provides information about what cookies are, how they are used, and how users can manage cookies.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files placed on your browser by websites. These files help websites recognize your browser and remember certain information. Cookies are used to enhance website functionality and personalize the user experience.

Types of Cookies

The types of cookies used on our website are as follows:

  • Essential Cookies: These are cookies necessary for the website to perform its basic functions. Without these cookies, some sections of the website may not work.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies analyze how you use our website. They collect user behavior to enhance the performance of our website.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies remember user preferences and settings. They are used to personalize the user experience and make the website user-friendly.
  • Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to show ads that may interest you. They track your browsing habits and provide targeted advertisements.

Managing Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies by changing your browser settings. Disabling cookies may cause some functions of our website to not work properly. You can adjust your cookie settings from your browser’s privacy or security settings.

Updating Our Cookie Policy

This cookie policy may be updated from time to time. In case of any changes, the updated policy will be published on our website. It is recommended to review our cookie policy regularly.
