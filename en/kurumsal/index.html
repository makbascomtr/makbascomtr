About Makbaş

The Leading Export Brand Since 2001

Makbaş LTD. ŞTİ. entered the business world in 2001, solidifying its position in the industry as an example of entrepreneurship. Our company initially started by providing services in the field of trading and quickly grew to become an export-oriented business. Today, we operate in the construction and cosmetics industries, offering high-standard products and services in both sectors.

Our success in the export field is the result of our focus on quality and customer satisfaction. Our company has always prioritized quality to establish a strong presence in international markets. The journey we began with the motto "Quality Brands and Reliable Service" has been shaped by our principle of making customer satisfaction our core value. In line with this principle, we act with the responsibility of being a bridge between producers and consumers.

Makbaş has consistently met the competition in its sector with high standards, and since 2008, it has succeeded in being among the top exporters. Our innovative and disciplined work approach is one of the most important characteristics that set us apart from other players in the industry. We have achieved success in the countries to which we export by always prioritizing customer satisfaction without compromising on quality or service.

The vision of our company is to be recognized as the most reliable and high-quality brand on the global stage. To achieve this goal, we continuously follow and implement the latest innovations in the industry. Our mission is to keep customer satisfaction at the highest level at all times, while closely monitoring the most recent developments and trends in the sector. As Makbaş, we aim to continuously improve our business processes and exceed customer expectations.
